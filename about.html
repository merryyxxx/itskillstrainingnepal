About IT Skills Training Nepal

Empowering Nepal's future through cutting-edge IT education and practical training programs that transform careers and lives.

Building Nepal's Digital Future

IT Skills Training Nepal was founded with a vision to bridge the digital skills gap in Nepal and prepare our youth for the global technology landscape. We believe that with the right training and guidance, every individual can achieve their career dreams in the IT sector.

Our institute specializes in practical, hands-on training that ensures students don't just learn theory but gain real-world experience. From Graphic Design to Web Development, SEO to Digital Marketing, we offer comprehensive programs that are designed to meet industry standards and demands.

What sets us apart is our commitment to not just teaching, but nurturing careers. Our students receive certifications, paid internship opportunities, and ongoing career support that helps them transition smoothly into the professional world.

Our Mission

To provide world-class IT training that transforms lives and creates opportunities for Nepal's youth to compete globally while contributing to the nation's digital transformation.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's leading IT training institute, recognized for excellence in education, innovation in curriculum, and success in placing students in rewarding careers.

Our Values

Excellence in education, integrity in all dealings, innovation in teaching methods, and commitment to student success. We believe in practical learning, continuous improvement, and creating lasting impact.
